Zeo

Genre:

3D Action/Fantasy - My first 3D project.

Gameplay:

Basic "W-A-S-D" keyboard movement, "SPACEBAR" to jump, "L-Shift" and "Tab" keys for special abilities, "Alpha NUMs" for abilities selection & mouse controls.

Enemies fight differently, make sure to learn the controls and your special abilities.

Description:

Zeo is a 3D action/fantasy game with 4 different locations to play in. The character has multiple special abilities and a Godmode with basic keyboard & mouse controls.

Take a little bit of time to learn the controls properly and if necessary, press "ESC" to pause and take a look there for extra information or simply a reminder on what to do.

Each location has puzzles, each puzzle unlocks a collectible and these collectibles will progress the game further.

Requirements:

No specific requirements. The game is fairly light.
